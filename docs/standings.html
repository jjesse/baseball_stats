Standings Glossary

Basic Standings Terms

W: Wins - The number of games the team has won.

L: Losses - The number of games the team has lost.

PCT: Winning Percentage - Calculated as wins divided by total games played.

GB: Games Behind - The number of games a team is behind the division leader.

Divisions

AL East: American League East - Yankees, Red Sox, Blue Jays, Rays, Orioles

AL Central: American League Central - Guardians, Tigers, Twins, Royals, White Sox

AL West: American League West - Astros, Mariners, Rangers, Angels, Athletics

NL East: National League East - Phillies, Braves, Mets, Nationals, Marlins

NL Central: National League Central - Brewers, Cubs, Cardinals, Reds, Pirates

NL West: National League West - Dodgers, Padres, Diamondbacks, Giants, Rockies

Other Terms

Closest Division: The division where the gap between first and second place is smallest.

Best Record: The team with the highest winning percentage in all of MLB.
