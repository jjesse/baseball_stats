Understanding baseball standings and key metrics.

W (Wins)
Total number of games won by the team
L (Losses)
Total number of games lost by the team
PCT (Winning Percentage)
Wins divided by total games played (W + L)
GB (Games Behind)
How many games behind the division leader a team is
Division Winner
Team with the best record in each of the 6 divisions automatically makes playoffs
Wild Card
Top 3 non-division winners in each league make playoffs as wild card teams
